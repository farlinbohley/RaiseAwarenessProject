The Typical neighborhood of China

The urban villages are labyrinths and extremely complex, which Professor Ning Niu describes as “a lack of necessary infrastructure” and an “extreme uncoordinated urban landscape”

Considering its complexity and the lack of open data on the buildings, major navigation companies are reluctant to gather specific information about the Urban Villages. Therefore, many delivery drivers face difficulties navigating the last few hundred meters of their delivery

The Sina News had a article addressing the specific problem. Measures prior to my project have been taken to address this problem; however, efforts remain limited to posting a map of the neighborhood at the entrance, which can be hard to find.
